\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This class handles the methods to perform. 


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Approximation through a gaussian process(\+G\+P) of an underlying tumor model
\item Computation of maximum stiffness regions on posterior of GP in order to find tumor centroids
\item Computation of points attributed to tumor contour by exploration of Centroids
\item Spline approximation of tumor contour 
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
